# Definite Clause Grammars

Goals

1. introduce `Context Free Grammars`

## Context free grammars

finite collection of rules which tell us that certain sentences are grammatical.

|non-terminal symbols|->|symbols|
|:-:|:-:|:-:|
s|->|np vp
np|->|det n
vp|->|v
det|->|_a_
det|->|_the_
n|->|_woman_
n|->|_man_
v|->|_shoots_
